Udon

Description

Udon, a traditional Japanese soup that is popular throughout many communities

Ingredients

  • Udon Noodles
  • Pork Spare Ribs (Cubed)
  • Shoyu
  • Hondashi (Fish Stock)
  • Hawaiian Salt
  • Green Onions
  • Shitake Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Boil spare ribs and leave for about 1 hour

  2. Add Shoyu, Hondashi, Hawaiian Salt, Shitake Mushrooms to the soup base. Add to taste.

  3. Bring water to boil, then turn to low heat and leave for 2 hours

  4. Add Green Onions to broth

  5. Boil Udon Noodles

  6. Serve all in one bowl and Enjoy!
